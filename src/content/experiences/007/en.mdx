  reason: |
    After doing well in my first semester, the lab lead senior invited me to join.
    I had spent many nights studying alone in the club room, and the senior noticed that effort and recommended me.

  startingContext: |

  gains: |
    It became one of the most meaningful parts of my undergraduate years because I learned many things I could not have learned from classes alone.
