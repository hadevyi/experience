eventOperation:
  overview: |
    The 1st SSAFYnity Autumn Sports Day was an offline alumni event held on October 22, 2022 at Ttukseom Hangang Park Soccer Field 2.
    It was a participatory sports day with icebreaking, team games, cheering, and a closing event.
  background: |
    SSAFYnity prepared the event to help alumni meet in person beyond online connection.
    Following the founding ceremony, it became another offline moment where the alumni association expanded into a community that creates real relationships.
  role: |
    I served as the pre-event MC, helped open the atmosphere, and guided participants before the main program started.
    I also supported corner guidance and on-site staff work so participants could follow the game flow smoothly.
  operation: |
    I supported participant guidance, corner operation, crowd control, and on-site movement flow.
    When participants were injured during the sports day, I helped with simple first-aid support using the event first-aid kit.
  collaboration: |
    I worked with the SSAFYnity 1st executive team to align the event flow and shared on-site updates with corner leads.
    Since we also needed to coordinate with the SSAFYnity office and rental vendors, timing and communication flow were part of the preparation.
  challenge: |
    An outdoor event had more variables than an indoor event, including weather, movement flow, crowd distribution, and safety response.
    It was challenging to keep the energy of the event while still giving necessary guidance and control.
  result: |
    The event became an offline community moment where many SSAFYnity alumni met in person and actively joined team games.
    It helped the alumni association grow into a community where people could build real relationships.
  learning: |
    I learned that community events are not just about programs, but about designing moments where participants can naturally talk and move together.
    I also experienced that outdoor events require facilitation, guidance, and safety response to be designed together.
