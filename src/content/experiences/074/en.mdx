eventOperation:
  overview: |
    The 1st SSAFYnale was an IT conference held on June 10, 2023, at Multicampus Yeoksam.
    Alumni speakers shared their growth and career concerns as developers.
  background: |
    The event helped SSAFYnity expand from social networking into a technical community where alumni could share practical experience.
  role: |
    As the planning director of the 2nd SSAFYnity executive team, I led the overall event planning.
    I handled speaker coordination, timetable design, registration form setup, and registration responses.
  operation: |
    The event included alumni and guest talks, pre-submitted questions, recorded content, and job-group networking.
  collaboration: |
    I worked with speakers, operators, and stakeholders to align presentation flow and on-site operations.
  challenge: |
    It was my first time operating an IT conference rather than only attending one, so I had to define the format and operating rules from the organizer's side.
    The event required clear rules for talk flow, audience movement, questions, and recording.
  result: |
    The event recorded 54 total participants and became a reference point for alumni-led technical conferences.
  learning: |
    Conference-style events require content quality, speaker experience, and audience flow to be designed together.
    I also felt the limitation of keeping the event alumni-only, which led me to consider opening later conferences or IT events to external participants.
