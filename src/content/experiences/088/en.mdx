  overview: |
    SSAFYnity Fall Sports Day was an offline event held on September 28, 2024 at the Ichon Hangang Park soccer field.
    The program included team-based activities such as O/X quiz, tail tag, dodgeball, relay bingo, and relay race.
  background: |
    This was a regular sports event prepared to help SSAFYnity alumni members connect offline.
    For the 2024 event, I contributed a background game idea to keep participants engaged throughout the event.
  role: |
    Before the event, I handled registration, participant list management, and payment tracking as the HR team lead.
    On the event day, I supported on-site operations as staff and served as the MC to keep the overall flow and energy steady.
  operation: |
    Game planning and supplies were handled by other teams, while I focused on registration, list organization, and payment confirmation.
    On-site, I supported participant guidance and program flow.
  collaboration: |
    The HR team handled registration, participant lists, payments, and participant communication, while other teams prepared games and supplies.
    During the event, I helped connect each team's preparation into a smooth on-site flow.
  challenge: |
    The largest variables were the weather and outdoor environment.
    It was also challenging to maintain participant energy over a long event while serving as MC.
  result: |
    The event led to many participants converting to full membership and supported continued offline networking among alumni members.
  learning: |
    I learned again that outdoor events require not only registration and payment preparation, but also active handling of on-site variables and event pacing.
