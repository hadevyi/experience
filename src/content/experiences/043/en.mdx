    DND is a nonprofit IT organization for developers and designers.
    It operates with team-building systems, project-making guidelines, and educational support so participants can build services together.

    It was founded to share technical knowledge and seminar opportunities beyond Seoul-centered communities. Under the slogan "Enjoy projects, give opportunities to everyone," DND builds a culture where developers and designers collaborate on side projects over eight weeks.
