    I served as the head of the Culture Department and worked on detailed planning and promotion for department cultural events.

    Within the larger event direction set by the executive team, I suggested detailed sections and created promotional materials.
    The most memorable contribution was designing the department student council logo. Seeing that logo used on the department jacket made the work especially meaningful to me.
  challenges: |
    As a department head, I learned that the role was not only about executing what had already been decided. It was also about refining the details inside a broad guide.
    I had to ask what the executive team wanted to achieve and think about how the event would actually run on-site.
    Since I still had limited experience operating group events, I sometimes felt flustered when unexpected confusion happened in the field.
  impact: |
    This experience gave me more practice in planning and operating events.
    I learned to think beyond ideas and promotional materials, and to consider how people would actually move through and experience an event on-site.
