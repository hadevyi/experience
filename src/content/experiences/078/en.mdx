eventOperation:
  overview: |
    The 2nd SSAFYnity Autumn Sports Day was an outdoor sports event held on September 23, 2023.
    It was designed for alumni to interact through team activities.
  background: |
    The event continued the 2022 autumn sports day and helped establish it as a recurring SSAFYnity event.
  role: |
    As planning director of the 2nd SSAFYnity executive team, I planned the detailed activity flow and on-site operations.
    On the day of the event, I also served as MC and guided the flow of each activity.
  operation: |
    The event required preparation for clothing guidance, rain plans, cancellation rules, after-party guidance, fees, and refunds.
    We removed activities that had caused too many injuries in the 2022 event and redesigned the program around team activities that could be safely run with limited on-site staff.
    It included warm-up activities and team games such as group-forming games, color-flipping, dodgeball, and relay missions.
  collaboration: |
    I worked with the executive team to coordinate participant guidance, movement flow, scoring, first-aid readiness, snacks, and beverages.
    We also arranged an after-party venue so most participants could continue socializing after the sports day.
  challenge: |
    Because it was an outdoor event, rain and on-site safety were major concerns.
    Participant changes also affected team-based operations, so the flow had to remain adjustable on the day.
  result: |
    The event recorded 37 total participants and helped continue the autumn sports day as a recurring alumni event.
    Hearing that participants continued meeting after the event showed that the sports day worked as a meaningful alumni networking experience.
  learning: |
    Outdoor community events require program design, safety, movement flow, and participant changes to be considered together.
    I learned that the event host needs to manage both the atmosphere and the operational flow while balancing fun and safety.
