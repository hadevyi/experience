community:
  overview: |
    DND cohort operations support developers and designers so they can complete side projects together.
    I started participating in cohort operations on December 21, 2022, beginning with the 8th cohort.
  motivation: |
    I had experienced how difficult it can be to continue a team project as someone preparing for a developer role and still new to project work.
    I wanted to support DND participants with developer-oriented feedback and operations so they would not have to struggle alone when they got stuck.
  role: |
    As a development operator, I focused on helping teams complete their projects.

    Participating cohorts:

    - 8th cohort (January-February 2023)
    - 9th cohort (July-August 2023)
    - 10th cohort (January-February 2024)
    - 11th cohort (July-August 2024)
    - 13th cohort (July-August 2025)
    - 14th cohort (January-February 2026)
    - 15th cohort (July-August 2026)
  contribution: |
    I helped manage the recurring operation flow from recruitment, document review, team building, and orientation to ongoing feedback, mid-term presentations, final presentations, and retrospectives.
    While respecting team autonomy, I supported developer feedback, team communication, and career-related conversations when teams needed help.
  challenges: |
    Each team had different goals, expectations, and levels of engagement, so one operation style could not fit every team.
    Since multiple teams progressed at the same time, I needed to read each team's pace and intervene at the right moment.
  impact: |
    I became more comfortable communicating with many participants and operating a community at scale.
    I was able to closely observe participants complete their projects and continue growing within DND and the developer community.
