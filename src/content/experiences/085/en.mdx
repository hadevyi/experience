eventOperation:
  overview: |
    The DND hackathon was a public recruiting hackathon held from May 17 to 18, 2024.
    A separate event TF was formed for operation, while developer organizers supported participants with feedback on-site.
  background: |
    The event was designed to introduce DND more broadly and help participants implement real ideas within a short time.
    For me as an organizer, it was also my first time preparing and running a hackathon directly.
  role: |
    I joined the TF from the early preparation stage and worked as overnight event staff during the event.
    As the finance lead, I handled supply purchases, receipt processing, supply movement, and placement.
    I also used design team assets to create and print award panels.
  operation: |
    On-site, I managed supplies and space placement so participants could focus on development.
    As a developer organizer, I checked each team's progress and gave feedback so they could prioritize the core service flow first.
  collaboration: |
    The event TF handled preparation and on-site operation, while developer organizers helped participants move their projects forward.
    Because the roles were divided, I moved between operational work and development feedback depending on the situation.
  challenge: |
    The event ran overnight for two days, and I joined as staff after work, so the physical burden was high.
    Since hackathon teams needed to implement within a short time, I also had to help narrow priorities when teams designed too many unnecessary features.
  result: |
    I heard that the hackathon led some participants to apply for the next DND cohort, and some actually joined afterward.
    From the organizer side, it was meaningful as an event that introduced DND and expanded participation experience.
  learning: |
    The hackathon reaffirmed that building the core flow users can experience immediately matters more than trying to complete every feature.
    I also experienced the less visible operational elements of an event, such as finance and on-site logistics.
