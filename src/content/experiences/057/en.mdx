  overview: |
    Safers was an environmental metaverse project where users could meet extinct or endangered animals and complete missions related to environmental issues. Users entered the virtual world with a character and experienced an animal encyclopedia, missions, community features, and an animal matching personality test.
  background: |
    It was developed over six weeks as a SSAFY 5th autonomous project. The team was formed by people who wanted to work on a socially meaningful topic, and we chose endangered animals and environmental issues as the subject we cared about most. As metaverse and remote content became more relevant after COVID-19, the team wanted to turn the issue into an experience that users could directly explore and remember instead of only receiving it as a heavy message. To avoid leaving it as a demo-only topic, we also reached out to and communicated with the National Institute of Ecology and IUCN to examine whether it could be operated as a real service.
  scope: |
    The service included Kakao login, user management, character setup, Unity-based shared and personal virtual worlds, board and comment/like features, an animal encyclopedia, mission logs, a non-member personality test, and image-based mission validation. The Unity WebGL build was connected to the web service and deployed through AWS EC2, Jenkins, and nginx.
