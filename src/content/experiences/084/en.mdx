eventOperation:
  overview: |
    The 2nd Night of SSAFY People was an SSAFYnity networking event held on February 17, 2024 at Hapjeong Tiramisu's Hapjeong main branch.
    It brought together alumni members and related participants, with a photo wall, team building, games, awards, and sponsor introduction.
  background: |
    This was one of the first large events run under the 3rd executive team structure.
    Since a sponsor introduction segment was added, on-site operation needed to help participants follow the event flow naturally.
  role: |
    After participating in event ideation and brainstorming, I focused mainly on my HR team lead role.
    Before the event, I handled registration, attendee list management, payment checks, and refund guidance.
    On the event day, I supported entry guidance and on-site operation.
  operation: |
    I organized the participant response flow in advance and checked entry movement and guidance needs on-site.
    I also supported the operation so the event could proceed in the planned order.
  collaboration: |
    Sponsorship coordination and detailed planning were handled by other members, while the HR team focused on participant management and on-site guidance.
    The event structure and on-site operation were divided across teams.
  challenge: |
    A venue change created variables in on-site movement and guidance.
    I needed to adjust guidance so participants could enter and take part without confusion in the changed space.
  result: |
    Participants were satisfied enough to continue gathering after the event on their own.
    It was meaningful that exchange continued naturally as an alumni networking event.
  learning: |
    I learned that registration and on-site guidance at a large event are not minor support tasks, but part of the operational flow that shapes the participant's first experience.
    I also learned the importance of on-site operation when responding to unexpected variables such as venue changes.
