  overview: |
    SSAFYnity is an alumni community created by SSAFY graduates to stay connected after completing the program.
    It cooperates with SSAFY, but it is operated independently by graduates.

    SSAFY had produced many graduates, and the idea behind SSAFYnity was that it would be a shame for those connections to simply disappear after graduation.
  reason: |
    I wanted to keep the meaningful relationships I formed through SSAFY.
    I felt that networking among many developers was important, and I wanted to contribute to sustaining those connections.
  startingContext: |
    I started participating in SSAFYnity in March 2022.
    The initial members were recommended with help from the SSAFY office, and I was recommended because I had been active during SSAFY.

    The office helped gather people, but the alumni association itself was planned and operated by the executive members.
  challenges: |
    Because the community connected graduates from different cohorts and regions, building sustainable operations and participation flow was important.
    Beyond simple socializing, it needed to become a network where alumni could continue meeting and helping one another.
  gains: |
    This marked the start of my experience operating a developer alumni community after SSAFY.
    It later became the foundation for multiple executive roles and presidency, giving me long-term experience in connecting people through community operation.
