community:
  overview: |
    SSAFYnity 1st executive team was the initial operating team of the SSAFY alumni community.
    I joined as the first planning director from March 2022 to December 31, 2022.
  motivation: |
    I felt that SSAFY alumni needed a community to stay connected after completion and joined the initial operations.
    Recommended members who wanted to participate gathered to form the alumni association's first executive team, and I took on the planning director role.
  role: |
    I participated as the first planning director.
    The first organization was divided into three divisions: planning, promotion, and operations. The executive team consisted of three chair members and the director/deputy director of each division, for a total of nine people.

    The planning division was the largest division with about 25 members, and it mainly handled the event and operation planning needed in the early stage of the alumni association.
  contribution: |
    We built the operating structure and flow of the alumni association from scratch.
    I mainly planned early events such as the founding ceremony and autumn sports day, coordinating required items with the SSAFY office and Samsung Electronics.

    As planning director, I aligned event direction, preparation items, schedules, and collaboration methods with the planning division so early alumni activities could turn into actual events.
  challenges: |
    The hardest part was having to create everything without existing standards or precedent.
    The planning division had many members, and most participants were SSAFY graduates who were also working professionals, so discussions and work were only possible in the evenings or on weekends.

    It was not easy to align many people and opinions within limited time while also organizing points that required coordination with the SSAFY office and Samsung Electronics.
  impact: |
    This became the starting point for my long-term involvement in SSAFYnity community operations.
    In the first executive team, I gained experience building the initial operating method and event planning flow that allowed the alumni association to actually move.
