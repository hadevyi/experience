highlights:
  - Delivered on June 10, 2023 at the 1st SSAFYnale
  - Keynote introducing the purpose of SSAFYnale and the direction of the alumni developer network
  - Shared the event's purpose of exchanging SSAFY alumni experiences and technical highlights
mediaInterview:
  background: |
    I delivered the keynote for the first IT conference organized by the 2nd SSAFYnity executive team.
    Since I was responsible for planning the event, I also introduced the conference's purpose and preparation background to participants.
  context: |
    At the time, I was part of the 2nd SSAFYnity executive team and served as planning director.
    I was also involved in overall event planning, speaker coordination, timetable design, and registration operations.
    The keynote introduced the alumni group and explained why we were opening a tech conference centered on alumni members.
  topics: |
    The keynote introduced the meaning of SSAFYnale as a combination of SSAFYnity and Finale.
    It also framed the event as a place to share SSAFY alumni experiences and technical highlights, with the aim of growing it into an annual developer event.
  perspective: |
    I wanted to communicate that SSAFYnity could grow beyond a social alumni group into a technical community where members share experience and career concerns.
    The talk emphasized a network where job seekers and working developers could share their own steps forward.
  impact: |
    I received feedback that the talk helped even alumni members who were not familiar with SSAFYnity understand the community more clearly.
    It served as the opening talk for the later speaker sessions, replay content, and job-based networking.
