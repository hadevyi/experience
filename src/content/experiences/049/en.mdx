  - Always-available online Mogakko space

learningGrowth:
  reason: |
    Because SSAFY 5th cohort was run online during COVID-19, classmates had fewer chances to naturally get close or study together. I had heard that previous cohorts actively studied and worked together by class, so I started Mogakko as a way to create that rhythm online.
  startingPoint: |
    As the class representative of SSAFY 5th Cohort Seoul Class 13, I created a Discord-based online space where classmates could join anytime and study together.
  learning: |
    Instead of following one fixed curriculum, each person studied what they needed. Some prepared for jobs, some worked on SSAFY assignments, and others focused on personal study while staying connected on Discord and asking questions when needed.
  challenge: |
    Since it was hard to gather naturally like an offline class, the key was to make the space feel low-pressure rather than forcing participation.
  connection: |
    The activity naturally ended with the first semester, but it helped classmates become closer and build relationships where they could ask each other questions more comfortably. Even after class groups changed in the second semester, many people who had studied together in the first semester later worked together on projects, and some of those relationships have continued to this day.
