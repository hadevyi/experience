highlights:
  - SSAFYnity official founding event on June 9, 2022

  - Prepared the run sheet, rehearsals, on-site guidance, and hosted some program segments
eventOperation:
  overview: |
    The SSAFYnity founding ceremony was the first official event announcing the launch of SSAFYnity, an alumni association created by SSAFY graduates.
    It was held on June 9, 2022, from 19:00 to 21:00 at Multicampus Yeoksam 18F, with both online and offline participation.
  background: |
    The event was created to build a foundation where SSAFY alumni could stay connected after graduation.
    It was prepared as a free event open to alumni members.
  role: |
    As a member of the 1st executive team and the planning division lead, I oversaw the overall event planning.
    I asked division members to prepare detailed plans for each program segment, approved the final plans, and aligned the overall event flow.

    Before the event, I handled role assignment, run sheet preparation, participant guidance, and rehearsal.
    On the event day, I helped with setup and participant guidance, and also hosted some program segments.
  operation: |
    Offline attendees entered the venue after check-in, and the on-site experience included refreshments, a photo zone, and participant gifts.
    The main program included congratulatory videos, remarks, an introduction of the first executive team, memory videos, and a quiz show led by the planning division.
    Networking was then operated so that both online and offline participants could join.
  collaboration: |
    I worked with the SSAFYnity 1st executive team to align event announcements, attendee check-in, on-site flow, and online participation.
    Within the planning division, responsibilities were divided by program segment, and each plan was coordinated into the overall event flow.
    We also worked with promotion and operation members so pre-event notices and on-site responses could connect smoothly.
  challenge: |
    As an early founding event, many operating standards had to be created from scratch.
    The hybrid format required considering on-site guidance, online streaming, Webex networking, and participant flow at the same time.
    It was my first time operating an event with more than 100 participants, so there were many variables to prepare for across the before, during, and after-event flow.
  result: |
    The event became the starting point for SSAFYnity's official activities.
    Alumni participated both on-site and online, and many shared that they were glad the alumni association had finally begun.
    The event served as a foundation for later SSAFYnity events and alumni community operations.
  learning: |
    This event showed how important launch messaging, registration flow, and on-site operation structure are for a sustainable community.
    It also showed that designing both offline and online participation experiences helps more members enter and stay connected to the alumni community.
    I also learned that large events require not just good ideas, but role assignment, run sheets, rehearsal, and on-site response to be managed as one continuous flow.
